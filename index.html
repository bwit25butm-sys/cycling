About the Competition

Cit-E Cycling is a national cycling initiative that encourages healthy lifestyles and friendly competition. Participants compete using high-tech exercise bikes that accurately measure power output and distance travelled during one-hour sessions.

Individual Riders

Challenge yourself against cyclists from across the country and qualify for the national championship.

Cycling Clubs

Enter as a cycling club and compete for the highest combined power output and distance travelled.

Live Performance

Every ride records your power output and total distance to determine the overall rankings.
